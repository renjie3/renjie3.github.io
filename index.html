Jie Ren

I am a Postdoctoral Associate at MIT CSAIL. I obtained my Ph.D. degree from Michigan State University under the supervision of Professor Jiliang Tang, and my bachelor's degree from Tsinghua University.

My research interests lie in Trustworthy and Interpretable AI, with a focus on data and model protection in Generative AI.
